HOW DOES THE PURCHASING PROCESS WORK?
It’s pretty straightforward. After finding the artwork and artists you connect with—and having the opportunity to speak one-on-one with creators—you’ll purchase whichever piece of artwork you’re drawn toward through Unfiltered Expressions. Then you’ll share your shipping details with us so we can coordinate directly with the artist to have the artwork sent to you. All shipping costs will be covered by you.
