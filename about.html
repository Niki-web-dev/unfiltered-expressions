HI. we’re NoAH aNd GRAnt.

AND THE ENTREPRENEURIAL HUSTLE HAS BEEN WITH US EVER SINCE THE LEMONADE STAND
We crossed paths at the University of Denver and slowly stumbled into the art space after connecting with a ton of artists who wanted to digitize their art through NFTs.

But the more artists we met, the more we realized how many barriers there are that prevent talented people from bringing their work from studios to galleries.
Because not only is it hard for artists to get into galleries without a stamp of approval from big-name collectors or celebrities, but once inside those galleries, creators are subjected to high commission rates (50-60%) and rigid contracts.

And, creators are rarely given the opportunity to share their stories and inspiration with buyers, either.

We started Unfiltered Expressions with
the humble and eager hope to do things differently.

To artists, we dedicate ourselves to giving exposure to those who have been undervalued and unseen, to paying creators 70% instead of 40-50%, to doing away with contracts that limit artists to selling through one gallery, and to building a digital space where creatives can share the inspiration behind their creations.
And for buyers and referral partners, our goal is to provide an experience void of gallery snottiness, to open the door to deeper connections with artists and artwork, and to create an online market where art enthusiasts of all backgrounds can share their love of creativity.
We are beyond honored to do this work and have such a deep appreciation for every single person, including you, for being here.

we win,

That saying stems from its own meaningful story.

SIMILAR TO EVERY PIECE OF ARTWORK WE ARE PROUD TO SHARE

It was a saying that Grant’s grandfather, Ronald (Ron) Brady used to say all the time about what it took to have a winning performance.

And just like understanding where that saying comes from brings a deeper significance to it, so does hearing an artist share the why and how of their artwork.

When you know the backstory of the brushstrokes, a piece of artwork grows to represent a story; a life.

That’s the type of connection we hope we can help you have
with every piece of art you buy here.
