WIll DaY’s bACksTORy 

Born in New York City, William Day started his career in finance (although, looking back, he recalls that the first time he ever felt like an artist was at the age of 13). After 9/11, when Will’s wife, Aimee, survived the collapse of the World Trade Towers, he left his finance job and began studying architecture. But he still felt like something was missing. So he began painting at night, after work—and realized that creativity was what he had been longing for.
Will paints with inspirations from his thoughts: experiences, passions, views, moods; all of which shape his meaning and perception. The work he creates invites us into a world where our own stories come alive in the way we view his abstract art.

With a studio space in Boulder, Colorado, Will Day welcomes buyers and art enthusiasts who are eager to experience his art firsthand and hear the meaningful stories behind each creation.

CAREER MILESTONES

Some of Will Day’s exciting career milestones include a commissioned painting of Peyton Manning for the Denver Broncos and features in top magazines like LUXE, Forbes, and Whitehot (to name a few). He continuously inspires others to chase after their own dreams through inspiring seminars and his TED Talk titled, “Finding Creativity in Chaos.”

stYLe + PRoceSS

Will Day works with unprimed large-scale canvases, creating layers and textures with colorful oils and acrylics. His paintings are abstract, energetic, and all nod to the intensely emotional and nuanced human experience.

A few weeks later, we reached out to Will to discuss the creation of Unfiltered Expressions; an online art gallery that would do away with so many of the challenges Will had spoken of.
‍
With his support and enthusiasm toward being our very first artist, Unfiltered Expressions was born.
